Tech Empower Africa

Our Mission

At Tech Empower Africa, our mission is to shine a spotlight on organizations dedicated to empowering young girls in Africa by providing them with opportunities to thrive in the field of technology. We are committed to showcasing and amplifying the remarkable work of these organizations, inspiring and supporting the next generation of female tech leaders across the continent.

SheCode Africa
She Codes Africa is a dynamic and groundbreaking organization at the forefront of a transformative movement aimed at empowering women in the technology sector across the African continent. Founded with a vision to bridge the gender gap in tech and elevate women's participation in the digital era, She Codes Africa has emerged as a trailblazing force in promoting diversity, inclusivity, and innovation within the tech ecosystem.

Africa Gilty Girls in Tech
Africa Gilty Girls in Tech takes a holistic approach to women's empowerment, recognizing the significance of both technical skills and leadership development. The organization's comprehensive training programs span cutting-edge tech disciplines, such as software development, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and blockchain, enabling participants to stay at the forefront of the rapidly evolving tech landscape.

Tech Train Incubator Foundation (TTIF)
Techy Train Incubator Foundation is a dynamic and forward-thinking organization that has swiftly emerged as a catalyst for innovation and tech entrepreneurship with a strong emphasis on social impact. Guided by a mission to empower aspiring tech innovators and change-makers, the foundation offers a comprehensive and transformative platform for nurturing talent, fostering creativity, and driving positive change in communities across the globe.

Women Techsters
At the core of Women Techsters' mission lies a commitment to providing women with comprehensive and accessible opportunities for tech education and skill development. Through a range of specialized training programs, coding boot camps, and workshops, the organization equips women with cutting-edge skills in areas such as web development, data science, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. By investing in continuous learning and professional growth, Women Techsters ensures that women have the necessary expertise to thrive in an increasingly technology-driven world.

Girls4Tech Youth for Technology Foundation
Girls4Tech Youth for Technology Foundation places a strong emphasis on inclusivity and reaching underserved communities. By actively collaborating with schools, community organizations, and NGOs, the foundation ensures that girls from diverse backgrounds have access to its programs. Through scholarships and outreach initiatives, Girls4Tech strives to break down financial barriers and provide equal opportunities for all girls to explore their potential in technology.
